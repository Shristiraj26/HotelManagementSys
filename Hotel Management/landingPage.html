Welcome to The Hotel Ellite, Hyderabad

Experience wellness at our luxury hotel in the City

Located in the heart of Raheja Mindspace IT Park, The Hotel Ellite Hyderabad is a luxurious hotel with 427 rooms catering to business and leisure travellers. The hotel in HITEC, Hyderabad, offers state-of-the-art convention facilities under the Marriott Bonvoy portfolio, with 20,000 sq. ft of event spaces that can host up to 700 guests. The lap pool, complete with an in-built bar, is the perfect spot to unwind after a long day. With six exceptional and award-winning restaurants, guests can choose from various global food and beverages. Whether you prefer Peshawari specialities at Kangan, Italian delicacies at Prego, or global and Asian cuisine at Seasonal Tastes, the selections will surely delight your taste buds. The hotel is conveniently located just a 40-minute ride from the Hyderabad International Airport and is minutes away from the IT Parks in the city, making it the perfect choice for any business traveller looking to make the most of their itinerary and experience all that the city has to offer.
